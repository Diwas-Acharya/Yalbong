About Our Homestay

Nature of Yablong is situated in a beautiful village of Kalimpong hill district, West Bengal. Our homestay offers an off-beat location away from the hustle and bustle of city life, allowing you to reconnect with nature.

Located near Siliguri, we are easily accessible from NJP Railway Station and Bagdogra Airport while still providing the authentic village experience you seek.
